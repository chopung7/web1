Mother Care

Mothercare plc is a global brand for parents and young children with a 58-year history as a specialist UK retailer.

We are now focussed on global franchise operations through its subsidiary, Mothercare Global Brand (MGB), in Europe, the Middle East and Africa, Asia and Latin America.

Across the global business there are over 800 stores in over 40 territories where our products are sold through MGB's franchise partners' territories in retail stores and through a small but growing online presence.
